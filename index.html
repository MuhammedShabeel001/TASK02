About vcpkg

vcpkg is a free C/C++ package manager for acquiring and managing libraries.
hoose from over 1500 open source libraries to download and build in a single step
or add your own private libraries to simplify your build process. Maintained by the
Microsoft C++ team and open source contributors.

Simplicity and flexibility are central to vcpkg.

Easy To Use

Download and build popular libraries in a single step. Never worry about
upstream dependencies or version conflicts. Declare dependencies with
commandline actions or in a manifest that can live with your repo. Get
support from the development team, documentation, and an active
community.

Universal

vcpkg works with your preferred OS, buildsystem, target architectures,
IDE, editor, and continuous integration process. Libraries are built from
source and can be configured.

Reliable

Maintain consistency between local developer and CI/CD workflows. Stay on your preferred library versions. Sync your dependency ecosystem with the rest of your team

For Everyone

Whether you are a one-person team on a small open source project or a large enterprise with complex build and security needs, take advantage of the latest vcpkg features for your workflow. Cache binaries for faster consumption, avoid diamond dependency resolution issues, reproduce identical builds for all developers, CI machines, and containers. Bring your own libraries or consume popular public ones. Take advantage of existing Visual Studio and Visual Studio Code integration.
